# Better control when adjusting your fixed prices in {{resource:Common_Product_Name_Variable_Advisor}}

Now you can be even more precise when it's time to adjust your prices in Spiris. We have listened to your requests and added a filter for specific tasks in the bulk update fixed prices wizard. This means you no longer have to search through long lists when, for example, you only want to increase the price for year-end closing or another specific service. By filtering for exactly what you need, you get a better overview and can feel confident that the correct prices are being updated.

In the first step of the wizard, you simply select the task you want to focus on in the new filter. The list then shows only the fixed prices where the selected task is included. Even if a fixed price contains several different tasks, it will be included in the selection so that you can smoothly adjust the whole. This saves valuable time and gives you full control, especially when you want to make targeted price adjustments for specific parts of your offering without affecting the rest of your subscriptions.
