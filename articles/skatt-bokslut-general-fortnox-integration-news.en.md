# Year-end closing just got easier – discover our new Fortnox integration!

**We are now launching a direct integration between Spiris and Fortnox for those working in **{{resource:common_product_name_variable_taxation_year_end_closing}}**. By connecting the platforms via API, you can now handle both the import of accounting data and the export of year-end closing events completely seamlessly. The feature is available to all users and makes your work more efficient from start to finish.**

We always strive to make your financial work as smooth as possible. Previously, you had to handle SIE files manually to move data from Fortnox to Tax & Year-end Closing. With our new integration, that step is completely eliminated. Instead, a secure and direct link is created that saves time, reduces the risk of manual errors, and gives you better control over the numbers.

### Why you will love this update

*   **Say goodbye to manual files:** Forget exporting and importing SIE4 files. Now, all accounting data is retrieved directly with just a few clicks.
*   **Automatic schedules:** When you import data, schedules for accounts receivable (account 1510) and accounts payable (account 2440) are automatically created. This gives you a head start on the reconciliation.
*   **Sync back to the source:** When the year-end closing is complete, you can send your year-end adjustments (V and S vouchers) directly back to Fortnox. You choose which voucher series they should be placed in.

### How it works

The integration is built to be as easy as possible to use:

1.  **Connect in the setup wizard:** When you start a new engagement, you will now find Fortnox as an option among the data sources.
2.  **Secure login:** You are redirected to Fortnox where you log in with your regular credentials and authorize the connection. Once completed, the connection remains active for 30 days and is automatically renewed every time you perform an import or export.
3.  **Smooth period reconciliation:** All transaction analysis and period closing work exactly as usual, but with the advantage that the data is always updated via the API.
4.  **Exporting vouchers:** Under the year-end adjustments view, you will find the option to export to Fortnox. In a simple dialog box, you select which series the vouchers should belong to, and they are then sent over directly without any complicated intermediate steps.

### Get started right away
You can find the new feature in the setup wizard for new engagements or under the data source settings in the maintenance menu. If you already have work in progress, you can easily switch the data source to Fortnox to take advantage of the direct connection.

We hope this news will provide you with a calmer and more efficient year-end closing period. More time for advisory and less time for file management – just as it should be!
