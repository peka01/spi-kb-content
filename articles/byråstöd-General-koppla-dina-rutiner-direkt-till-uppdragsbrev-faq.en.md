    ### Hur kopplar jag en specifik rutin till sidan för uppdragsbrev?

    Du hanterar kopplingen under kundinställningarna i plattformen. Där kan du
    välja vilka rutiner som ska vara synliga för olika delar av kundarbetet. När
    du har gjort kopplingen kommer rutinen att finnas tillgänglig via
    rutinknappen direkt på sidan för uppdragsbrev.

    Alla användare som har behörighet att arbeta med kundens uppdragsbrev kan se
    de kopplade rutinerna. Det gör det enkelt för hela teamet att hålla sig
    uppdaterade om vilka instruktioner som gäller för just det arbetsmomentet.

    Ja, du kan välja att visa de rutiner som är relevanta för att hantera
    uppdragsbrev på ett korrekt och smidigt sätt. Målet är att du ska slippa
    leta efter instruktioner i andra dokument och istället ha allt samlat på ett
    ställe.

# Link your routines directly to engagement letters

We want to make it as easy as possible for you to work correctly and efficiently in {{resource:Common_Company_Name_Short}}. Therefore, we have now added the ability to link your internal routines directly to the engagement letter page, just as you have previously been able to do for the system overview and powers of attorney.

### How does it work?
By linking a routine to engagement letters, you and your colleagues get quick access to your shared instructions and workflows exactly when you need them. This reduces uncertainty and ensures that all steps in the process are performed correctly.

### Where can I find the feature?
You can find the settings for linking routines under the tab for ****{{resource:NavigationCustomerMenu}}****. Once a routine is linked, it will be displayed when you click the button for routines on the **Engagement Letter** page.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I link a specific routine to the engagement letter page?
You manage the link under the client settings in the platform. There, you can choose which routines should be visible for different parts of the client work. Once you have made the link, the routine will be available via the routine button directly on the engagement letter page.

### Who can see the routines linked to the engagement letters?
All users who have permission to work with the client's engagement letters can see the linked routines. This makes it easy for the entire team to stay updated on which instructions apply to that specific task.

### Can I link multiple routines to the same page?
Yes, you can choose to display the routines that are relevant for handling engagement letters in a correct and smooth manner. The goal is for you to avoid searching for instructions in other documents and instead have everything in one place.
