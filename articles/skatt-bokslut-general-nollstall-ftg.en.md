# Reset company in **{{resource:common_product_name_variable_taxation_year_end_closing}}**
## Overview
Sometimes you may need to start over from scratch with a company in Tax & Year-end Closing. This could be because incorrect data has been imported or you have used the company to test various features in our financial platform. By resetting the company, you clear all existing information and get a completely empty workspace to start from.

## Step by step
Follow these simple steps to reset the information for the selected company:

1. Ensure you are in the company you want to reset in Spiris.
2. Navigate to the company settings menu.
3. Locate the option to reset the company.
4. Confirm that you want to perform the reset. 
   * **Note:** This is a permanent action that deletes all data registered for the company in these functions. Therefore, be careful to check that you have selected the correct company before confirming.
5. Once the process is complete, the company is reset and you can start working with a fresh start.

## Good to know
- **Security:** Since the reset cannot be undone, it is wise to double-check that any important documentation is saved elsewhere if it might be needed later.
- **Use cases:** The function is particularly convenient if you want to clear test data or if you want to perform a completely new import of accounting values without old settings remaining and interfering.
