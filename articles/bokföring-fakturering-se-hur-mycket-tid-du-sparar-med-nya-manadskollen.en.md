## See how much time you save with the new **{{resource:PrintOutType_MonthlyReport}}**

Now **{{resource:PrintOutType_MonthlyReport}}** has become even sharper to help you understand the true value of your digital work. Through new insights for both sales and purchasing, you can see exactly what percentage of your invoices are handled fully digitally via services like Autoinvoice and Kivra compared to traditional methods. The best part is that you now get your time savings in black and white; Spiris automatically calculates how many hours and minutes you save by avoiding manual handling and mail delivery, so you can spend that time developing your business instead.

To make it easier to access the statistics, the notification that the report is ready now remains for a full 14 days under the clock icon, instead of disappearing after just one day. You will find the report directly in your to-do list at the beginning of each month and can click through at your convenience to follow your progress toward a paperless cash flow whenever it suits you best during the first two weeks of the month.
