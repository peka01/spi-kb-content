# New: Easier archiving and export of your fiscal years

We are now launching a long-awaited feature in **{{resource:common_product_name_variable_taxation_year_end_closing}}** that makes it easier than ever to save and archive your year-end closing history. You can now export complete documentation for one or more fiscal years simultaneously to a well-structured ZIP file.

This update is designed to help you meet legal requirements for data storage and provides you with a secure, human-readable backup of all your work in the platform.

### Everything gathered in a smart folder structure
When you perform an export, the platform automatically creates an organized ZIP file. Inside the file, you will find a clear folder structure:
*   **Main folders** are named after each respective fiscal year.
*   **Subfolders** are created for each period.
*   **PDF documents** are placed in the period folders and contain all relevant documentation, such as automatic checks, markings for completed accounts, and your own comments.

To make the export as relevant as possible, only periods where you have actually performed work or left comments are included.

### How to do it
You can find the new feature by navigating to **Maintenance** – **Export data** in the menu.

1.  Select the **Documentation** option.
2.  Select the fiscal years you want to include in your export. You can choose a single year or multiple years at once.
3.  Click the button to start the export.

### Continue working while the export is being created
Since an export of multiple years can contain a lot of data, the process runs in the background. You will see a progress bar showing the progress, but you don't need to wait at the screen. You can continue working on other tasks in the platform in the meantime. Once the file is ready, you will receive a notification and can easily download your finished ZIP file.

### Available to everyone
This feature is available to all users, including those with a read-only subscription. This means you can retrieve your history at any time and save it locally or at the firm.

In the unlikely event that a document cannot be created, you will receive clear information about this via the notification system, so you can always trust that your archiving is complete.

We hope this update provides you with a more secure and time-efficient management of your fiscal years in Spiris!
