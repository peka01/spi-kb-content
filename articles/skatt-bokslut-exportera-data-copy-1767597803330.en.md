# Export accounting data for archiving

In **{{resource:Common_Company_Name_Short}}** **{{resource:common_product_name_variable_taxation_year_end_closing}}**, you can collect the company's accounting data for several financial years in a structured ZIP file. This feature is designed to make it easy for you to comply with the Accounting Act's requirements for seven years of archiving, for example, when a subscription is terminated or when a company transitions to read-only mode.

## How to do it

1. Go to the ****{{resource:Menu_title_Maintenance}}**** menu and select ****{{resource:ExportData_title_ExportData}}****.
2. Select the ****{{resource:ExportData_option_Documentation}}**** option.
3. Select the financial years you want to include in the export from the ****{{resource:Startpage_hdr_FinancialYear}}**** list. You can choose to export a single year or multiple years at once.
4. Click the ****{{resource:Maintenance_ExportData_hdr_Export}}**** button.
5. A progress bar is displayed while the export is being prepared. You can continue working in the platform in the meantime.
6. When the file is ready, you will receive a notification. Click the ****{{resource:DocumentationExport_Notification_Download}}**** link in the notification to save the ZIP file to your computer.

## What does the export contain?

The export creates an organized folder structure that makes it easy to find the right documentation in the future:

*   **Years and periods:** The ZIP file contains main folders for each financial year, and inside these are subfolders for each period.
*   **PDF documents:** All relevant documents are saved as PDF files in the period folders. This includes appendices with auto-checks, accounts marked as completed, and documents where comments have been provided.
*   **Full history:** The export also includes data from periods with specific markings or notes, ensuring that important information is preserved.

## Tips
> If the export is only partially successful (for example, if a specific document has an incorrect format), you will receive a notification about this. You can then see which document it concerns and handle it manually to ensure that your archive is complete.

## More information
*   **Archiving requirements:** According to the Accounting Act, accounting information must be saved for seven years. This export helps you secure your data locally if you no longer plan to have an active subscription in Spiris.
*   **GDPR:** Keep in mind that the Accounting Act takes precedence over GDPR regarding the storage of personal data in accounting documentation. The data must therefore be saved throughout the entire archiving period.
*   **Read-only mode:** The feature is also available for companies that are in read-only mode.
