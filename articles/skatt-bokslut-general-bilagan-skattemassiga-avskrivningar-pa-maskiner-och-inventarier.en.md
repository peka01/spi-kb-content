# Manage tax depreciation on machinery and equipment

This appendix helps you keep track of and calculate the company's tax depreciation for machinery and equipment. By using the appendix, you ensure that depreciation is carried out according to current tax regulations, providing you with a secure and accurate basis for the year-end closing and tax return.

## How to do it

1. Navigate to the attachments view in your financial platform and choose to add or open the attachment ****{{resource:TaxCalculations_Txt_RefKey_TAXRESIDUALVALUEMACHINEINVENTORY}}****.
2. Check that the opening tax value matches the previous year's closing value.
3. Fill in details about the year's acquisitions (purchases) and disposals (sales or retirements) in the relevant fields.
4. Use the built-in calculations to compare ****{{resource:TaxCalculationSE_RealEstateTaxValue_hdr_Huvudregeln}}**** (the 30% rule) and the **Supplementary Rule** (the 20% rule). 
5. Select the depreciation option that is most advantageous for the company to determine the lowest permitted tax value.
6. When you are finished, ensure the attachment is marked as completed to include the values in your year-end closing work.

## Tips
> You can easily switch between the main rule and the alternative rule directly in the appendix. This allows you to quickly see which rule results in the lowest residual value and thus the largest tax depreciation for the year.
