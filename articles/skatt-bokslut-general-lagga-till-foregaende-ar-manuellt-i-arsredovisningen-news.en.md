# Now you can add previous year's figures manually

Have you ever sat with an annual report and realized that the comparative figures for the previous year are missing because the accounting was not entered into the platform? Now you can avoid that obstacle. We have made it possible for you to manually enter the previous year's figures directly into the annual report, even if you haven't imported any accounting data or SIE files for that year. This is a long-awaited update for those who have recently started using Spiris or for other reasons lack history in the platform, but still need to comply with the Annual Accounts Act and show correct comparative figures for your clients or your own company.

You activate the feature under the basic information for the annual report by checking the box **Show previous year**. Once the setting is made, an extra column appears in the income statement, the balance sheet, and in your notes where you can fill in the values yourself. To make the work even smoother, you can select the function to show all rows in the tables so that you see exactly where the figures should be placed. This way, you can always complete and submit a full annual report that meets all legal requirements, without having to struggle with old import files after the fact.
